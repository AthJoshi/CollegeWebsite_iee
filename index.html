About My College

This Group of Institutions offer 64 Undergraduate, 35 Postgraduate, 16 Integrated, 2 Research programmes and 2 M.Tech Industrial Programmes. In addition, full-time Ph.D. in Engineering and Management disciplines, Ph.D. in Science and Languages and Integrated Ph.D. programmes in engineering disciplines are offered.

Our Institution has a strong international presence across the world and academic partnerships with over 440 foreign universities. It provides options to study 2 years on campus and 2 years at a partner foreign university. One may also study a semester abroad or engage in international research collaboration activities with partners.
